Yuan Dong is a PhD candidate in the Philosophy Department at Duke University. Her research primarily focuses on epistemology, including virtue epistemology, social epistemology, and formal epistemology. She also has a strong interest in Chinese philosophy and neuroethics.

In her spare time, Yuan enjoys exploring local jazz clubs, immersing herself in the rich sounds of jazz music. At home, she relaxes by playing games like Overwatch or the Soulsborne series while spending time with her beloved cats, Gui and Pi.
